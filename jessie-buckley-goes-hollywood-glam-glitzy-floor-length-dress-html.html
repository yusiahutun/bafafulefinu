Jessie Buckley goes full Hollywood glam in a glitzy floor length dress

Jessie Buckley went full Hollywood glam as she stepped out at the Screen Actors Guild Awards on Sunday night.

The actress, 32, donned a show stopping silver floor length dress for the event lauding film and television stars.

Jessie's frock featured a stylish tie at the waist, revealing her hourglass figure.

She swept her red locks into a side parting, wearing them in soft waves. The star added a few rings to her glamorous outfit, keeping her accessories classic. 

She grinned and pouted as she prepared for the fun event along with a host of other big names. 

Glitz and glam: Jessie Buckley went full Hollywood glam in a glitzy floor length dress as she stepped out on the red carpet on Sunday night

Gorgeous girl: She swept her short red locks into a side parting for the event, wearing them in soft waves

It comes after Jessie spoke candidly about her struggles with anxiety, saying she now embraces the bad days after panic attacks 'scared her' as a teen.

The Chernobyl star told Evening Standard Magazine she still suffers from good and bad days, and now has regular visits with a therapist to handle her issues.

Speaking about her struggles with mental health, Jessie that she's in a good place now after battling with panic attacks as a teen.

She said: 'It’s definitely better, but I’m still human. I still have days when I get sad. And I get days when I get happy, and I get days when I get scared.

'But now I feel the things. I’m not frightened of them. And actually they’re all important. You’ve just got to accept them, invite them in and say, ''Have a cup of tea and what’s going on?'', instead of trying to repress them.

'Which I probably did when I was young, 'cause I was scared of them.'

 The Irish actress added that she now has regular visits to a therapist, which despite her busy acting schedule help her in overcoming her mental health issues.

Pretty: Jessie's frock for the star-studded event featured a stylish tie at the waist, revealing her stunning hourglass figure

Looking good: The star added a few rings to her glamorous outfit, keeping her accessories classic

She added: 'It’s harder [to do] when you’re away filming, because you have to build that relationship up.

'But I b****y love it. It’s been brilliant for me. It’s about being honest. There’s no point pretending. If I don’t do that in my own life, how the hell am I meant to play somebody else’s?'

While Jessie spoke openly about her battles with mental health, the War and Peace star was coy when asked about her ex-boyfriend and former co-star James Norton at the time.

She added: 'Ah, I prefer not to talk about that! You just make choices. My private life is very normal. I live with a group of friends, I go buy milk...'
