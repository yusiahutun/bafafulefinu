How to choose the best natural moisturizer for your skin

Because natural face moisturizers tend to harness essential oils to hydrate and treat skin, it's important that you look for a formula geared toward your specific skin type—especially if you're acne-prone, sensitive, or oily. That's because essential oils can sometimes exacerbate skin issues, so it's best to look for a formula that has been tailor-made for your skin.
