11 Best Red Lipstick Shades of 2024

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Image courtesy of Fenty Beauty

Best Celebrity Favorite Red

MAC Cosmetics Matte Lipstick in Ruby Woo

Best Celebrity Favorite Red

MAC Cosmetics Matte Lipstick in Ruby Woo

Credit: Ulta
Pros
  • Non-feathering for up to 10 hours

Arguably the most famous red lipstick shade, MAC’s semi-matte is well loved by celebrities like Tracee Ellis Ross. Its culty formula was even spun off into a version made just for Rihanna, aptly named Riri Woo.

Customer review: “Love the color. Mac products stay on longer and the lipstick is creamy and keeps lips moist longer!” —Cheryl

Best Liner and Lipstick

Nars Velvet Matte Lip Pencil in Dragon Girl

Best Liner and Lipstick

Nars Velvet Matte Lip Pencil in Dragon Girl

Credit: Space NK
Pros
  • Easy to apply
Cons
  • Must be used with a sharpener for best results

Here’s an unapologetically bright color from NARS that falls somewhere between classic red and vibrant pink. It’s no wonder Dragon Girl’s unique’s shade is a favorite of the red lipstick queen herself, Taylor Swift.

Customer review: “The Velvet Matte Lip Pencils are a staple in my pro artist kit. I’ve had them for years. They are quite literally one of my ride or die products. They are ALWAYS the first product I reach for when doing lips and they are perfect for sanitizing and having in the set bag without mess.” —Crazypretty

Best Long-Lasting Red

Fenty Beauty Stunna Lip Paint Longwear Fluid Lip Color in Uncensored

Best Long-Lasting Red

Fenty Beauty Stunna Lip Paint Longwear Fluid Lip Color in Uncensored

Credit: Fenty Beauty
Pros
  • Highly pigmented
  • Non-feathering formula

Rihanna’s first red lipstick under her Fenty Beauty label is not only universally flattering, but also easy to apply. Once you swipe it on, its matte formula will last until you decide to wipe it off at the end of the night.

Customer review: “I love this! I only have to apply it once and it never budges! Wear it all day, eat, drink and never have to reapply. I always get compliments when I wear it. Color is beautiful, my favorite!” —ErwinK

Best All-Around Red

Dior Rouge Dior Lipstick in 999

Best All-Around Red

Dior Rouge Dior Lipstick in 999

Credit: Saks Fifth Avenue
Pros
  • Includes a number of finishes

Dior’s iconic 999 lip shade has had its fair share of celebrity fans over the years. It also helps that the lipstick looks beautiful on every skin tone, and nicely lasts up to 16 hours per wear.

Customer review: “I love everything about this lipstick. Wears wonderfully all day. Creamy, smooth, luxurious.” —MickellsMom

Best Cool-Toned Red

Chanel Luminous Intense Lip Colour

Best Cool-Toned Red

Chanel Luminous Intense Lip Colour

Credit: Chanel
Pros
  • Features a satin finish

There are a dozen perfect red shades in Chanel’s arsenal, but Pirate is by far the most fun to say. It uses sappan wood to keep lips smooth and hydrated, with a satin finish that’s not overly shiny.

Customer review: “I have long been a fan of red lipsticks so when Chanel introduced this new formula and I saw the gorgeous array of reds, I just had to give some a try! Passion is truly a drop dead beautiful red and good for any time of the day. Pirate is a bit darker and for some might be more of a evening color. Coromandel is a warmer bright red and very wearable. Palpitante is a delicious creamy feeling red with a fuschia cast - quite flattering and ladylike! If you are looking for a high quality lipstick, this formula and color variety will not disappoint!” —Tropical Traveler

Most Cult-Favorite Red

Violette_FR Petal Bouche Matte in Amour Fou

Most Cult-Favorite Red

Violette_FR Petal Bouche Matte in Amour Fou

Credit: Violette_FR
Pros
  • Non-feathering
  • Long-lasting

Here's a matte red from Violette_FR with a creamy, velvety texture that won't dry out your lips. While all three of the brand's Petal Bouche shades are worth adding to your collection, the fiery Amour Fou in particular is a red you'll want to wear in any season.

Customer review: "I love a bold lip. This goes on smooth and moist, and stays put for hours." —Alisha M.

Best Warm-Toned Red

Gucci Rouge à Lèvres Mat Matte Lipstick in Goldie Red

Best Warm-Toned Red

Gucci Rouge à Lèvres Mat Matte Lipstick in Goldie Red

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Hydrating

Although a newer formula than others on this list, once you jump for Gucci’s signature red—especially in this gilded tube—you’ll never look back. The ultra-soft matte color can be blurred on your lips for a just-there red, or layered on for an opaque pop.

Customer review: “Love this lipstick. The formula feels soft and doesn’t dry out your lips but looks matte. The Goldie red has such a beautiful payoff. The packaging is also very beautiful. I never right reviews but was very impressed. Can’t wait to wear this out!” —TheyCallMeStacC

Best Orange-Red

Pat McGrath Labs MatteTrance Lipstick in Obsessed!

Best Orange-Red

Pat McGrath Labs MatteTrance Lipstick in Obsessed!

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Features a comfortable matte formula

Fans of this orange-red lipstick swear it’s the best matte formula ever made. In addition to featuring a creamy, smooth formula that never feels drying, it’s also nicely formulated for one-stroke coverage.

Customer review: “This is one of the best lipsticks, I’ve ever bought. The rich color and texture makes for a nice matte finish with a vibrant color that lasts.” —Ininih

Best Red for Pigmented Coverage

Beauty Bakerie Lip Whip Matte Liquid Lipstick in Mon Cheri

Best Red for Pigmented Coverage

Beauty Bakerie Lip Whip Matte Liquid Lipstick in Mon Cheri

Credit: Nordstrom
Pros
  • Smudge-proof

Beauty Bakerie’s smudge-proof Matte Lip Whip is a favorite among makeup enthusiasts, since it stays put all day—even after eating and drinking. Plus, this fire engine red shade also happens to be both vegan and waterproof.

Customer review: "I am a connoisseur of lipstick from $10 tubes to $50 tubes, all to achieve, “The desired look”. This is, hands down, my favorite lipstick. Long lasting, rich colors! Wish Nordstrom carried more colors from this line. The cranberry color is breathtaking!" —Desh93

Best Satin Finish Red

YSL Rouge Pur Couture Lipstick in Le Rouge

Best Satin Finish Red

YSL Rouge Pur Couture Lipstick in Le Rouge

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Hydrating

Over 600 Sephora customer swear by this signature crimson shade from YSL. You’ll need only a few swipes of the satin finish lipstick to make a bold makeup statement that lasts up to six hours per wear.

Customer review: “Very pigmented and stays on all day. Doesn’t transfer to your teeth, which I have a problem with sometimes. I have the matte version too, but this one stays on longer and feels better to wear. Most pigmented lipstick I’ve had.” —clarrisse

Best Eco-Friendly Red

Hermès Rouge Hermès Satin Lipstick in 64 Rouge Casaque

Best Eco-Friendly Red

Hermès Rouge Hermès Satin Lipstick in 64 Rouge Casaque

Credit: Neiman Marcus
Pros
  • Refillable

It’s only natural to envision the color orange when you think of Hermès, but the brand has a nice range of other kinds of red lipstick options, too. Bazaar editors reach for their Rouge Casaque shade the most, a radiant red inspired by garments worn by professional jockeys.

Customer review: “Beautiful luxury lipstick! I love how it looks on me (yellow, cool tone). It is not drying but it is not long-lasting. I’m a makeup collector so this is a perfect addition to my collection. The box and packaging is lovely!” —Mimiloveswreslting
